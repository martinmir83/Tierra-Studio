01 introduction

Hello and Welcome to TIERRA – A Space for Clay, Creativity, and Community.
At TIERRA, we believe in the beauty of handmade ceramics and the connection between earth, hands, and creativity. Our studio is a space where beginners and experienced potters alike can explore the art of pottery, refine their skills, and express themselves through clay. Whether you’re looking to take a class, join an open studio session, or simply be part of a creative community, TIERRA offers a warm and inspiring environment to shape, create, and connect. Join us in discovering the joy of working with clay—one piece at a time.

03 about us

Tierra isn’t just a pottery studio—it’s a reflection of Noemi and Martin’s journey, their love for travel, and their deep connection to the earth. They first met in Vietnam when travelling the world and spent first years of their life together living in Barcelona where Noemi’s passion for pottery began. The name “Tierra” feels perfect, capturing both the essence of clay and the roots they’ve planted together. At Tierra we focus on hand modelling which gives each piece such a unique and organic feel—there’s something special about the slight imperfections and individuality of every item. Each piece evolving naturally through the process. We are combining both functional and decorative ceramics that allows you to create pieces that are not only useful but also artistic expressions.

04 classes

Beginners Hand Building Class (6 weeks) £150

There’s a deep sense of satisfaction in making something with your hands. Watching a raw lump of clay transform into something functional or beautiful. In this 6 week course we will guide you through the basics of hand building. You will learn about different techniques to hand build your pottery such as pinching, slab rolling and coiling. Throughout the course, you’ll also discover different ways to add texture and decoration, with the final sessions dedicated to glazing and firing your work for a small aditional fee. Whether you come with your own vision or prefer to be guided step by step, these two-hour sessions are designed to nurture creativity and offer a calming, hands-on experience in a supportive environment.

Taster Hand Building Class (2 hours) £50

Discover the joy of working with clay in our 2-hour hand-building taster class—perfect for beginners and creative explorers! In this relaxed and fun session, you’ll learn the fundamental techniques of hand-building, such as pinching, coiling, and slab building. This class is perfect for complete beginners or anyone looking to explore their creativity with clay. We’ll guide you through the process step by step, from shaping your mug to adding unique textures and details. This class is a great way to try pottery before committing to a longer course. All materials and tools are provided. If you’d like to keep your piece, we’d be happy to fire and glaze it for you (£10 extra per piece).
We are trying to be as environmentally friendly as we can and clay can be only recycled prior firing not after. So if you just want to experiment with clay and are not too keen on keeping your first creation please let us know and we can then recycle your piece

Hand Building Class With Kids (2 hours) £25

In this 2-hour session, children will discover the joy of working with clay while learning simple hand-building techniques. With plenty of guidance and encouragement, they’ll get to shape their own unique creations—whether it’s a fun animal, a special keepsake, or a quirky little bowl. This class isn’t just about making pottery—it’s about having fun, getting messy, and enjoying the magical process of turning clay into art! Pottery is also a wonderful sensory and mindful activity that helps children develop fine motor skills, patience, and self-expression. All materials are included. We charge addition £10 for glazing and firing should you wish to keep their finished masterpiece.

Clay & Wine Class (2.5 hours) £60

Unwind, get creative, and indulge in a relaxed evening of pottery and wine. This 2,5 hours hand-building session is the perfect blend of creativity and socializing—ideal for a date night, a catch-up with friends, or simply a unique way to de-stress. With a glass of wine in hand, you’ll learn fundamental hand-building techniques such as pinching, coiling, and slab construction, shaping your own beautiful ceramic piece. No experience is needed—just bring your enthusiasm, and we’ll provide the rest! All materials are included, and your finished piece will be fired and glazed, ready for collection at a later date. (We charge £10 extra per piece)

Kiln hire & firing services at
Tierra Pottery Studio

We offer a kiln firing service for those who just need access to a kiln. It’s ideal for home potters—whether you’re hand-building or throwing—who don’t have their own kiln. It’s also a great way to save on costs and energy by sharing kiln space. Our studio is equipped with a Nabertherm top 60L kiln, With a firing temperature of up to 1320°C, this kiln is ideal for medium-sized ceramic projects. We typically bisque fire to 900°C and glaze fire to 1250°C.
Costs
Bisc firing - 6£ per kilo (weighed before firing).
Stoneware firing - 8£ per kilo (weighed before firing).
Bisc Firing Full kiln hire fee £45 per firing.
Shared kiln hire fee £23 per shelf space per firing (up to 11cm height).
Stoneware Firing Full kiln hire fee £65 per firing.
Shared kiln hire fee £30 per shelf space per firing (up to 11cm height).
The fee includes technical support, loading and unloading the kiln.

05 membership

Starting your own pottery studio can be expensive. If you’ve fallen in love with pottery but aren’t ready to set up your own space, we invite you to share ours. Pottery is more than just creating with clay—it teaches patience, mindfulness, and the beauty of slowing down. Our studio offers a welcoming space where you can develop your skills at your own pace, unwind, disconnect, and let your creativity flow. Message us for more information—we’d be happy to show you around!
